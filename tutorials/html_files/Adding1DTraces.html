Adding 1D Traces

Adding 1D Traces

You can add 1D traces to your ND spectra which will be dynamically shown at your current mouse position. Simply type TH to toggle a horizontal trace on or off, or TV to toggle a vertical trace on or off. Alternatively right-click and toggle Horizontal/Vertical Trace on/off.

Change the scaling of the traces with TU (scale trace up) and TD (scale trace down).
